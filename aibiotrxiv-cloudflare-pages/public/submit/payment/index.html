Submission payment

US$5 processing or platform maintenance fee

For a first submission, this payment is a manuscript handling fee for editorial screening, platform maintenance, file storage, and administrative processing. Payment does not guarantee publication. AIBioTrXiv may still reject a first submission if the content is outside scope, clearly inaccurate, unsafe, poorly documented, or fails required declarations.

For post-publication versions v16 and later, the US$5 payment is a platform maintenance fee for publishing another preserved version. These later versions do not enter editorial screening, but reported violations may lead to account suspension and article unpublication.
