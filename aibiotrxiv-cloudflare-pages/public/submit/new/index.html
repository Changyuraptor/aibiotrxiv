Rights, credit, and record protection

AIBioTrXiv records creator credit, submission time, selected license, and a manuscript fingerprint so authors have a stable public record for AI Research papers and AI Idea submissions.

Author protection record: each formal submission receives a timestamped submission ID, member-account linkage, payment record, selected license, content fingerprint, version history, and visible credit statement. Idea submissions are labeled as ideas while keeping credit fully attached to the listed authors.

This record does not replace legal registration or legal advice, but it creates a traceable archive record that can help resolve credit, authorship, licensing, and priority disputes.
