Submit to AIBioTrXiv

Before submission, prepare your text section by section. Figures should be uploaded beside the relevant section as JPG or PNG files, with legends included.

1. Register and verify email

Every submitter needs a verified member account before preparing a formal submission. This keeps drafts, payment records, and editorial status connected to the author.

2. Build the manuscript draft

Prepare the title, abstract, author information, AI Research or AI Idea category, research area, section text, figures, and figure legends before formal submission.

3. Attach figures and legends

Figures should be uploaded beside the relevant section as JPG or PNG files. Legends must be included so the web article and PDF layout can be prepared correctly.

4. Choose rights and credit terms

Select the Creative Commons license and confirm author credit, timestamped record protection, version history, and reuse conditions before sending the manuscript forward.

5. Save as draft first

Manuscripts remain editable as member drafts until the author is ready. Drafts can be reviewed and corrected from the member dashboard before payment.

6. Pay US$5 to submit

Payment is the final step. The US$5 fee supports editorial handling, platform maintenance, storage, and administrative processing; it does not guarantee publication.
