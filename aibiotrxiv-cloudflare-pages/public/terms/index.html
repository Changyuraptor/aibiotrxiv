Terms of Use

By using AIBioTrXiv, members and readers agree to use the platform responsibly and to respect author credit, rights, and community rules.

Submission responsibility

Submitters are responsible for the accuracy, legality, originality, rights status, and biological claims of their submissions. AIBioTrXiv may reject, correct, unpublish, or remove material that violates platform rules.

Fees

The US$5 submission processing fee supports editorial screening, storage, and platform maintenance. Payment does not guarantee publication and may still be followed by rejection.

Licenses and reuse

Authors select a Creative Commons license during submission. Readers must follow the terms shown on the article page. AIBioTrXiv does not grant rights beyond what the article license and applicable law allow.

Peer comments

Peer comments must be constructive. Spam, harassment, personal attacks, impersonation, repeated off-topic comments, or escalating arguments may lead to removal or account action.

Administrative action

AIBioTrXiv may suspend accounts, remove comments, reject submissions, unpublish articles, or retain administrative records when needed to protect the archive.
